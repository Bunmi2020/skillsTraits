What is a proofreading skill

Proofreading skill is the ability to check text for errors and mistakes. Proofreading skill is a skill with a good eye for detail, grammar, and spelling. Proofreading skill is the ability to do final checks on written work before it goes to print or becomes live on a website.

A person who proofreads is a proofreader. They may specialize in specific fields or publications, such as business reports and academic works.

Where is proofreading skill being used

Proofreading skill is commonly used in the aspects of writing to crosscheck grammar, spelling, and punctuations. This skill is used in picking out questions of style, clarity, and structure. Proofreading skill is also used in academic work, to check the presentation of numbers, referencing, and the overall formatting of the work.
