What is concept art skill?

Concept art skill is the ability to convey an idea into visual art. Concept art skill is the ability to design and create art that visualizes a character, creature, or any creative assets. Concept art skill is a creative skill that gives attention to details of an idea of a product and design visualize character from the idea for producers to produce into the product. Concept art skills turn a brief idea into a new visual character.

Where is concept art being used

Concept art skill is used in creating a visual detailed design for images for the following:

  • Animation
  • Game
  • Movie production
  • Vehicle production
  • Clothing
  • Toys production
  • Fashion production
  • Graphic designing
  • Logo branding
  • Product designing
  • Weapon designing

Who is requesting for concept art skills

Almost all organization requires concept art skill, to convey the idea they have for their product into a visual image then proceed to production. Animation companies cannot do without concept art skills to create visual characters from a story they want to produce, also a movie and game companies. Even ordinary products we use day by day first go through the concept art stage, products like vehicles, clothes, chairs, houses, laptops, phones, luxury and fashion products, weapons, etc.

So, concept art skill is a high-demand skill across all organizations.
