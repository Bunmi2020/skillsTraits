What is a Graphic designing skill?

Graphic designing skill is the ability to create visual content to communicate messages that inspire inform and captivate consumers. Graphic designing involves the use of text and images. People with this skill are referred to as graphic designers.

Graphic designer makes use of computer software such as photo editing software, digital illustration, and layout software to create visual designs and elements such as logos, original images, and illustration to convey messages and deliver messages to the consumer and also to design layouts such as fonts, size, color, headings, and text.

Graphic designing is being used but not limited to the following:

It is used to create the production design and overall layout of the work. Graphic designer makes use of art and technology to create graphic for production illustration and logos. They determine how text and images will go together in prints and on a webpage.
