What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing skill is the ability to market products for organizations and it is paid based on performance. A person with this skill is referred to as an affiliate marketer. Affiliate marketers make use of the online platform to attract a specific audience and promote products and services by placing Adverts on websites, blogs, or Apps and earning a commission in return.

Where is Affiliate Marketing being used?

Affiliate marketing is being used for the following:

  • Advertisement
  • Promotion
  • Marketing
  • Product and Service Awareness
