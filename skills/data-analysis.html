What is data analytics skills?

Data analytics skills is the ability to analyze raw data to derive a conclusion about the information. Someone with this skill is referred to as a data analyst

Four types of data analytics skills are applicable in business, they are:

  • Descriptive analytic skills: the ability to answer questions about what happened and provides essential insight into past performance. It summarizes data in a meaningful descriptive way. It is used to track the success or failure of a business.
  • Diagnostic analytics skills: taking the descriptive analytics further and digs deeper to find causes. It is the analytics that helps answer questions about why things happened. It is used to investigate why a business is a success or failure.
  • Predictive analytics skills: predictive analytic is used to answer questions about what will happen in the future. Predictive analytics analyze past data to discover the trend and determine if there is going to be a reoccurrence. It is used to provide valuable insight into what may happen in the future of a business.
  • Prescriptive analytics skill: the ability to answer questions about what should be done. Prescriptive analytics make use of insight made from predictive analytics to derive a decision.

Where is data analytics skills being used?

Data analytics skill is important and is being used by businesses and organizations to succeed and to increase efficiency and improve performance. Data analytics is used in the field like:

  • Information technology
  • Statistics
  • Business
  • Accounting

Who is requesting data analytics skills?

Data analytics are required by organizations and corporations, and it is in high demand because it is important for the success of the business. Organizations request data analysts for the following reasons:

  • Data analyst helps an organization to make sense of raw data.
  • To determine Return on Investment(ROI)
  • To investigate and discover why business got better or worse.
  • To help business organizations to make informed and effective decisions
