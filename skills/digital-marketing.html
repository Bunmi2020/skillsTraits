What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing skill is the ability to sell products online. Digital marketing skill is process of conveying promotional messages and campaigns through electronics devices. It is a form of e-commerce. Digital marketing skill is the online marketing and promotion of brands and products to potential customers using the internet and other forms digital communication. It takes many forms like online videos, display adverts, social media posts, broadcasts etc.

People with this skill are refer to as digital marketer. Digital marketing make use of some tools to carry out effective marketing which are:

  • Google analytics
  • Facebook ad manager
  • Salesforce
  • Zapier
  • Mailchimp

Where is Digital Marketing skill being used?

Digital marketers are responsible for the following but not limited to:

  • Brand awareness
  • Product and service promotion
  • Social media marketing
  • Email marketing
  • Advertisement
  • Public relations
  • Customer relationship management
  • Pay-per-click adverts

Who are requesting for Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing is in high demand by many corporation and organizations because the world revolves around internet now and e-commerce is the way of marketing now. They demand for digital marketers to create content that will pull targeted audiences to the brand and products.
