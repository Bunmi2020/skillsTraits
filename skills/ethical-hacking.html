What is Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking skill is the ability to make use of authorized attempts to gain unauthorized access to computer systems, data, or applications. Ethical hackers are people with ethical hacking skills, they are security experts that identify a security vulnerability in a computer system or application and resolve it before a malicious attacker has the opportunity to exploit them. Ethical hackers make use of their security knowledge and ability to secure the technology of an organization.

There are a few ethical hacking concepts that an ethical hacker must follow:

  • Stay legal
  • Define scope
  • Report vulnerability
  • Respect data vulnerability

Where is Ethical Hacking being used?

Ethical hacking is used in Cyber security; find a weakness, find a way to remove the weakness, and ensure that the technology system is secure.

Who is requesting Ethical Hacking skills

All organizations making use of technological systems require ethical hackers for their cyber security. To secure their files and data from malicious hackers, to detect vulnerability and weakness in their system, and provide a way to protect the system.
