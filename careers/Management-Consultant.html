Who is a Management Consultant

Management analysts, often called management consultants, propose ways to improve an organizationâ€™s efficiency. They advise managers on how to make organizations more profitable through reduced costs and increased revenues. They often specialize in certain areas, such as inventory management or reorganizing corporate structures to eliminate duplicate and nonessential jobs.
